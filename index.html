Your first conversation with one of our qualified experts will be all about you and your home improvement project. We’ll ask some questions, confirm your information and schedule an at-home visit so we can put together a free quote for you.

Finalize your design and receive your personalized quote

During your in-home consultation, we’ll take measurements, evaluate the project, and provide a free quote valid for 1 year. During that time, we’ll also review options for materials, colors, upgrades and accessories, and review personalized financing or payment options.

5
Set your installation dates

Your installation date will be set once all custom-made products are ready for install. A Homeowner Experience Advisor will work with you to schedule an installation date that accommodates to your schedule.

6
Warranty and post-installation care

Our installers will arrive at your location, perform any removal of old materials, install new products, and clean up before leaving. While installation time can vary, we are often able to complete most projects in as little as one day! After the installation is complete, you’ll receive any warranty information and be encouraged to reach out with any follow-up questions.
